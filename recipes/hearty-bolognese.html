Hearty Bolognese

Description

A rich, comforting Bolognese made with minced beef, mushrooms, onion, leek, tomato, and slow simmered aromatics. The mushrooms add depth and a savoury note, while the leek gives the sauce a gentle sweetness.

Serves: 6 to 8 portions

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 1 hour 40 minutes


Ingredients

  • 900 g minced beef
  • 300 g fresh mushrooms, sliced or finely chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large leek, white and light green parts only, finely sliced
  • 3 to 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely grated or finely chopped, optional
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 700 to 900 ml tomato passata or tomato purée
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 100 to 150 ml water, stock, or red wine
  • 1 bay leaf, optional
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, thyme, or basil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • A small pinch of sugar, optional
  • A very small pinch of bicarbonate of soda, only if needed
  • 1 small knob of butter, optional, to finish
